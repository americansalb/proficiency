Welcome to the AALB Proficiency Screening

This screening will assess your language proficiency through a series of recorded questions.

⚠️ Camera & Microphone Required

This test requires access to your camera and microphone. Your responses will be recorded for evaluation purposes. Please ensure:

  • You are in a quiet environment
  • Your camera and microphone are working
  • You grant permission when prompted

English Proficiency Test Instructions

  1. You will listen carefully to questions you're asked in English, and you will respond to them, also in English.
  2. There are no right or wrong answers, rather, we're testing to see your ability to listen and speak in the English language.
  3. Please provide complete responses.
  4. You may repeat each question up to two times before responding.

Non-English Proficiency Test Instructions

  1. You will listen carefully to questions you're asked in English, and you will respond to them, but not in English!
  2. Rather, you will respond in your chosen non-English language (Spanish, French, Arabic, etc.).
  3. There are no right or wrong answers, rather, we're testing to see your ability to speak in your chosen language.
  4. Please provide complete responses.
  5. If you are fluent in multiple non-English languages, pick one for the purposes of the test. Your certificate will qualify for all languages you're fluent in, regardless of the one you chose for this test.
  6. You may repeat each question up to two times before responding.

There are a total of 5 questions. Once you are ready, click below to test your camera and microphone.
